# AUD-CAP: Corrective Action Plan

## Quick Reference
- **SLA:** 5-30 days (severity-based)
- **Team:** Internal-Ops (INT-)
- **Project:** Corrective Actions

## Required Labels
- `Type: bug`
- `Flag: compliance`
- `Layer: [affected area]`
- Severity: Major NC = High, Minor NC = Medium
